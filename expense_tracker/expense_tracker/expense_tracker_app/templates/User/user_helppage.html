Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) Section

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage,
fill in your details, and confirm your email address.

How do I add a new transaction?

Navigate to the "Add Transaction" section,
enter the details of your expense or income, and click "Save."

Can I edit or delete a transaction?

Yes, go to your transaction history,
select the transaction you want to edit or delete, and choose the appropriate action.

How do I generate a report of my expenses?

Go to the "Reports" section, select the time frame, and type of report you want,
then click "Generate Report."

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page,
enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I set up a recurring transaction?

When adding a new transaction, select the "Recurring" option and
specify the frequency (daily, weekly, monthly).

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is protected with encryption and secure authentication protocols.
We also offer two-factor authentication for added security.

Can I export my transactions to a spreadsheet?

Yes, you can export your transactions as a CSV file from the "Reports" section.

How do I contact support if I have an issue?

You can reach our support team via the "Contact Us" form
available on the help page or by emailing us at support@example.com.

How do I update my account information?

Go to "Account Settings" and update your profile, email,
or password as needed.
